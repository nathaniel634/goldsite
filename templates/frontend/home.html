About PMX

Who We Are

PMX Precious Metals is a premier, globally operating precious metals refinery, delivering world class purchasing, refining, minting, and assaying services for gold, silver, palladium, and diamonds.

With a strong commitment to integrity, security, and excellence, PMX serves clients worldwide, providing trusted solutions across the full precious metals value chain.

Built upon a foundation of integrity, innovation, and strict regulatory compliance, PMX Precious Metals ensures that all incoming materials whether recycled or newly mined are responsibly sourced, fully traceable, and aligned with the highest international standards.

Security

Specialized, comprehensive security measures at our refineries and during shipments to protect your valuable assets internally and externally.

Technology

Robust, high-extraction technology with state-of-the-art equipment, efficient handling, and environmentally friendly processes.

Ethics

Strict adherence to human rights, labor, environment, and business ethics. All materials we acquire are responsibly sourced and traceable.
