Precision-Crafted. Globally Trusted. Sustainably Refined.

Sam Precious Metals offers a complete range of investment-grade gold casted bars and grains, designed for investors, traders, and industrial users who demand both purity and performance. Refined in our advanced facilities and certified under UAE Good Delivery standards, all products are crafted from ethically sourced gold and produced with meticulous attention to quality.

Our casted bars are available in a variety of weights and purities—including 100g, quarter kilo, half kilo, one kilo, TT bars, and large bars—with fineness levels of 995, 999, and 9999. These bars are ideal for secure investment portfolios, high-volume trade, and institutional storage, offering both liquidity and long-term value.

Our gold grains—refined to 995, 999, and 9999 purity—are engineered for efficient use in jewelry manufacturing, coin minting, and industrial applications. Uniform in size and optimized for melt consistency, Sam grains are trusted by producers seeking high-quality input with seamless performance.

Whether for asset diversification or material sourcing, Sam Precious Metals delivers reliability, ethical production, and market-grade purity in every form of gold.
