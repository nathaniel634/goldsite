Love, Given Freely. Value, Held Forever.

Some moments can’t be captured with words—but they can be preserved in silver. The Bouquet of Heart minted bar by Sam Precious Metals is a tender expression of love, affection, and emotional truth. At its center lies the universal symbol of the heart—radiating compassion, joy, and devotion. A heart-shaped bouquet, delicately crafted, to embody the beauty of giving fully, freely, and without condition.

This design is more than ornamental—it speaks to the deeply human desire to connect and to cherish. Whether offered as a gesture of romance, friendship, or familial love, it is a symbol of presence and permanence. A silver reminder that true affection does not fade—it deepens with time.

Each bar is minted in-house at Sam Precious Metals’ world-class refinery, using ethically sourced silver and refined to the highest purity, certified under UAE Good Delivery. The result is a gift of both emotional and tangible value—a keepsake that holds meaning in every gram.

For weddings, anniversaries, milestones, or moments of gratitude, Bouquet of Heart is a tribute in silver to the people who matter most.
