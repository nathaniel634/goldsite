Dubai, UAE – November 25, 2025:

Sam Precious Metals, one of the Middle East’s leading accredited precious-metals refineries, has produced the world’s largest silver bar – an extraordinary Guinness World Record-breaking creation weighing 1971 kgs, reflecting the UAE’s founding year and engraved with the map of the country. The bar was unveiled at the Dubai Precious Metals Conference 2025 (DPMC), where it immediately drew global attention as a landmark achievement for the region and a striking demonstration of Dubai’s expanding capabilities in regulated precious-metals innovation.

Refined, cast, and finished entirely within Sam’s state-of-the-art facilities, the bar embodies a level of technical mastery rarely required in conventional precious-metals production. Its creation demanded a specialized casting mold engineered to withstand the weight and temperature of molten silver on an unprecedented scale, as well as precisely controlled melting and cooling phases to prevent internal stress fractures. Maintaining purity at record-breaking volume required highly coordinated workflow across metallurgical, engineering, and safety teams, followed by rigorous structural inspections to ensure stability and surface integrity. The result is a singular feat of craftsmanship and engineering that sets a new benchmark for the global precious metals industry.

The project brought together contributions from select regional partners, with Sam Precious Metals leading the initiative both in the full physical production of the world-record bar and in funding its creation. Tokinvest introduced the digital infrastructure that will allow investors to access regulated fractional ownership of the asset, and DMCC served as the ecosystem’s regulatory and trade facilitator, reinforcing Dubai’s established role as a global hub for commodities and precious-metals activities.

Reflecting on the achievement, Mr. Sami Abu Ahmad, Chairman of Sam Precious Metals, stated: “Producing the world’s largest silver bar represents a defining moment for our refinery and for the region’s precious-metals sector. The technical challenge behind this achievement was substantial, requiring deep coordination between our engineering and refinery teams to ensure purity, stability, and structural precision at a scale rarely attempted. This project highlights what becomes possible when specialized partners contribute their expertise toward achieving a shared milestone. We are proud to contribute this achievement to the UAE’s growing reputation as a global center for precious-metals excellence.”

In addition to its historical and symbolic importance, the bar will also be made available as a regulated digital investment through Tokinvest, allowing qualified investors to hold fractional representations backed by Sam’s verified physical production. This initiative aligns with Sam’s broader strategy to strengthen global trust in responsibly refined precious metals, supported by the
refinery’s own internationally recognized certifications, including UAE Good Delivery, the Responsible Jewellery Council Chain of Custody (RJC CoC), amongst others, and full adherence to OECD-aligned sourcing practices.

About Sam Precious Metals

Sam Precious Metals is a leading fully accredited precious-metals refinery headquartered in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, specializing in the refining, assaying, casting, and minting of gold and silver to the highest international standards. The company holds UAE Good Delivery accreditation for both gold and silver, along with the Responsible Jewellery Council Chain of Custody (RJC CoC) and Code of Practices (RJC CoP) certification, amongst others, and maintains strict compliance with OECD-aligned responsible-sourcing frameworks. With advanced refining technology, specialized die-creation and minting capabilities, and a strong commitment to ethical business practices, Sam Precious Metals serves global clients across investment, jewelry, industrial, and bullion sectors. The refinery is recognized for its commitment to transparency, quality, and innovation, and continues to contribute to the UAE’s growing prominence as a global hub for precious-metals excellence.

]]> In light of supporting the ‘We the UAE 2031’ vision, His Excellency, Dr. Thani Al Zeyoudi, the Minister of State for Foreign Trade and Minister in charge of Talent Attraction and Retention at Ministry of Economy, UAE, visited theleading gold refinery Sam Precious Metals, Dubai Headquarters recently.

‘We the UAE 2031’ vision

‘We the UAE 2031’ vision represents a national plan that seeks to enhance the position of the UAE as a global partner and an attractive and influential economic hub. The UAE is on course to achieve its target of Dh4 trillion ($1 trillion) in non-oil foreign trade by 2031.

The UAE accounts for a significant share of the world’s gold trade especially in the bullion trade.

The gold trade in the UAE is one of the most important economic sectors with a share of 20% of the country’s total non-oil exports.

H.E. Dr. Thani Al Zeyoudi, who is also the Chairman of Emirates Bullion Market Committee, made a visit to Sam Precious Metals, which is a UAE Good Delivery Refiner, and a highly renowned precious metals refinery in the region, mainly dealing in gold and silver.

Utilizing cutting-edge refining techniques and technology, Sam produces the highest quality of products that meet and exceed global standards. Sam products are actively traded in many exchanges, including Dubai Gold & Commodities Exchange (DGCX) and India International Bullion Exchange IFSC Limited (IIBX).

A range of local UAE banks, as well as other regional banks and regulated investment funds, deal in Sam gold and silver bullions.

Sam accreditation represents a comprehensive benchmarking system that encompasses all aspects of excellence, ranging from the Good Delivery to health, safety, environment, and management systems, as well as laboratory capabilities.

During his visit, H.E. Dr. Thani Al Zeyoudi, discussed with Mr. Sami Abu Ahmad, the Chairman of Sam Precious Metals, the forward-looking vision of His Highness Sheikh Mohamed bin Zayed Al Nahyan and the UAE’s leadership, and its approach to openness and collaboration with the rest of the world to drive sustainable economic growth.

H.E. Dr. Thani shared his views on UAE Cabinet’s approval, under the leadership of His Highness Sheikh Mohammed Bin Rashid Al Maktoum, of the policy to enhance the competitiveness of the UAE in the gold markets regionally and globally while maintaining its position on the world map as a global hub for gold trading.

H.E. Dr. Thani further mentioned about UAE’s plan to establish comprehensive economic partnership agreements (CEPAs) with nations and blocs that together account for 95 percent of global trade today, which will create more growth opportunities for the non-oil trade in line with UAE’s vision.

Mr. Sami, shared with H.E. Dr. Thani, the vision of Sam Precious Metals which involves an unwavering commitment to fostering connections between producing and consuming countries.

Sam understands the critical role that precious metals play in various global industries and strives to ensure a seamless and efficient flow of these valuable resources. Sam’s vision extends beyond traditional refining practices as it ventures into the realm of developing value- added products with wide-ranging applications.

Sam’s goal is to provide industries with high-quality materials that enhance efficiency, sustainability, and performance, contributing to advancements and breakthroughs across sectors.

Sam aims to be the top choice for customers in the precious metals industry, recognized not just for its products, but also for its integrity, reliability, and customer-centric approach.

Mr. Sami also talked about the challenges faced by the precious metals industry and provided insights into the future initiatives and developments that Sam is pursuing.

In addition, Mr. Sami shared with H. E. Dr. Thani the efforts taken by SAM towards digitalisation of its supply chain management systems and its advancements in responsible sourcing and AML/CFT compliance.

Mr. Sami and the leadership team at Sam provided H.E. Dr. Thani with a comprehensive tour of the Sam Refinery, during which H.E. Dr. Thani was able to review the refinery’s cutting-edge facilities and innovative design.

His Excellency Dr. Thani expressed appreciation for Sam Precious Metals’ exemplary achievements and pledged robust support from the UAE government to further expand the gold and precious metals industry.

Mr. Sami expressed gratitude to H.E. Dr. Thani for his kind words and promised that Sam Precious Metals will work relentlessly towards the growth and enhancement of the gold and precious metals sector in the UAE and globally.

On Friday 11th July, 2025, Sam Precious Metals was honored to welcome H.E. Luis Miguel Merlano Hoyos, Ambassador of the Republic of Colombia to the UAE, for an official visit to our refinery in Dubai – marking a key moment in the evolving trade relationship between our two nations.

On April 19th, 2024, Colombia signed a Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) with the UAE, its most important trading partner in the Middle East. A year later, both countries continue to build on shared ambitions to enhance trade, improve market access, and foster cooperation in sectors ranging from renewable energy, elimination of customs tariffs on a wide range of goods, and infrastructure to sustainable mining.

As one of the most reputable refineries, globally, Sam was selected for this visit in recognition of our technical capabilities, robust management systems, and deep understanding of how local brands can succeed on the global stage.

These strengths uniquely position us to support the long-term goals of the CEPA framework – not only in precious metals trade, but also in cross-regional knowledge exchange and engineering collaboration.
With internationally recognized Fairmined and Fairtrade certifications, Sam is also deeply aligned with Colombia’s commitment to environmental protection and ethical sourcing.

Our refinery’s ongoing work with traceable, responsible supply chains reflects a shared belief that trade and sustainability must move forward together.
At a time when global trade dynamics are shifting, the UAE-Colombia partnership signals a bold step toward diversified economic growth and opening access to the broader South American market, and specifically Latin America. Sam Precious Metals is proud to contribute its expertise, network, and vision to this next chapter of international collaboration.

Sam Precious Metals accueille l’Ambassadeur de Colombie pour renforcer le partenariat économique EAU–Colombie

Le vendredi 11 juillet 2025, Sam Precious Metals a eu l’honneur d’accueillir S.E. Luis Miguel Merlano Hoyos, Ambassadeur de la République de Colombie aux Émirats arabes unis, pour une visite officielle de notre raffinerie à Dubaï – marquant une étape importante dans l’évolution des relations commerciales entre nos deux pays.

Le 19 avril 2024, la Colombie a signée un Accord de Partenariat Économique Global (CEPA) avec les Émirats arabes unis, son principal partenaire commercial au Moyen-Orient. Un an plus tard, les deux nations poursuivent leurs efforts pour renforcer le commerce, améliorer l’accès au marché et encourager la coopération dans des secteurs tels que les énergies renouvelables, la suppression des droits de douane sur une large gamme de produits, les infrastructures et l’exploitation minière durable.

En tant que l’une des raffineries les plus réputées au monde, Sam a été choisie pour cette visite en reconnaissance de ses capacités techniques, de ses systèmes de gestion solides et de sa compréhension approfondie de la réussite des marques locales sur la scène mondiale. Ces atouts nous permettent de soutenir les objectifs à long terme du CEPA – non seulement dans le commerce des métaux précieux, mais aussi dans le partage des connaissances interrégional et la collaboration en ingénierie.

Certifiée Fairmined et Fairtrade à l’échelle internationale, Sam partage également l’engagement de la Colombie en faveur de la protection de l’environnement et de l’approvisionnement éthique. Notre travail continu avec des chaînes d’approvisionnement traçables et responsables reflète une conviction commune : le commerce et la durabilité doivent progresser ensemble.

À une époque où les dynamiques commerciales mondiales évoluent, le partenariat EAU–Colombie représente une avancée audacieuse vers une croissance économique diversifiée et un accès élargi au marché sud-américain, et plus particulièrement à l’Amérique latine. Sam Precious Metals est fière de mettre son expertise, son réseau et sa vision au service de ce nouveau chapitre de coopération internationale.

شركة سام للمعادن الثمينة تستقبل السقير الكولومبي لتعزيز الشراكة الاقتصادية بين الامارات و كولومبيا

يوم الجمعة الموافق 11 يوليو 2025، كان من دواعي شرف شركة SAM للمعادن الثمينة استقبال سعادة السفير لويس ميغيل ميرلانو أوجوس، سفير جمهورية كولومبيا لدى دولة الإمارات العربية المتحدة، في زيارة رسمية إلى مصفاتنا في دبي – مما شكّل لحظة مهمة في تطور العلاقات التجارية بين بلدينا

في 19 أبريل 2024، وقّعت كولومبيا اتفاقية شراكة اقتصادية شاملة مع دولة الإمارات، التي تُعدّ الشريك التجاري الأهم لكولومبيا في منطقة الشرق الأوسط. وبعد عام من توقيع الاتفاق، يواصل البلدان جهودهما لتعزيز التجارة، وتحسين الوصول إلى الأسواق، وتعميق التعاون في مجالات متعددة مثل الطاقة المتجددة، وإلغاء الرسوم الجمركية على مجموعة واسعة من السلع، والبنية التحتية، والتعدين المستدام

وباعتبارها إحدى أكثر المصافي شهرة على مستوى العالم، تم اختيار شركة SAM لهذه الزيارة تقديراً لقدراتها التقنية، وأنظمتها الإدارية القوية، وفهمها العميق لكيفية نجاح العلامات التجارية المحلية على الساحة العالمية. هذه المزايا تؤهلنا لدعم الأهداف طويلة المدى لاتفاقية CEPA – ليس فقط في تجارة المعادن الثمينة، بل أيضاً في تبادل المعرفة الإقليمي والتعاون الهندسي.

حاصلة على شهادات Fairmined وFairtrade المعترف بها دوليًا، تشارك شركة SAM أيضًا التزام كولومبيا بحماية البيئة والتوريد الأخلاقي. ويعكس عملنا المستمر في سلاسل التوريد المسؤولة والقابلة للتتبع قناعة مشتركة بأن التجارة والاستدامة يجب أن تتقدما جنبًا إلى جنب.

وفي ظل التغيرات الحاصلة في ديناميكيات التجارة العالمية، يمثّل الشراكة بين الإمارات وكولومبيا خطوة جريئة نحو نمو اقتصادي متنوع وتوسيع الوصول إلى السوق في أمريكا الجنوبية، وخصوصاً أمريكا اللاتينية. وتفخر شركة سام للمعادن الثمينة بتقديم خبرتها وشبكاتها ورؤيتها لهذا الفصل الجديد من التعاون الدولي
